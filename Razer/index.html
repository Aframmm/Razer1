RAZER INC.

Razer Inc. (stylized as R Λ Z Ξ R), is an American-Singaporean multinational technology company that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, financial services, and gaming hardware. Founded by Min-Liang Tan and Robert Krakoff, it is dual headquartered in one-north, Singapore and Irvine, California, US.
